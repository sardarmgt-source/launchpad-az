Our Story

Built by a
Student,
For Students

LaunchPad AZ was born from a simple frustration: Azerbaijani students had no tools built specifically for them. Foreign apps don't support AZN, don't understand our context, and are often too expensive.

We're building the ecosystem that we wished existed when we started university — a place where any student can manage money, launch ideas and build a career.

Built specifically for Azerbaijan
Free for all students
Community-first approach
